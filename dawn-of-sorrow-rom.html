The story follows after the events of Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow in the year 2036. A mysterious cult is planning to resurrect the evil lord Dracula, and Soma Cruz has to stop them. To protect the life of his loved one and to save himself Soma must enter the enemy’s home base, i.e. the replica of Draculas tower filled with mysterious creatures. You can defeat the enemy with the 'Magic Seal' system by using the touch screen. You can also exchange souls with other players with the help of DS wireless connection special effects. The main catch of Dawn of Sorrow and Aria of Sorrow is the same: Souls. Each enemy has one soul, and Soma has the power to take it. The enemy, after their defeat, sometimes give their souls to Soma. These souls have a variety of different users depending on the enemy who gave his soul. Some can reach out of the way items, and some can use offensive techniques.
