Step by step installation for the flip clock screensaver:

  1. Click the Fliqlo link.
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the page, and choose Download for Mac or Download for Windows.
  3. If a window doesn’t open automatically, search for the name “fliqlo_170” in Finder.
  4. Double click “Fliqlo.saver.” (A dialogue box may appear that warns you about opening applications from unidentified developers. At your own discretion, you can control+click or right click “Fliqlo.saver” and select ‘Open’ from the menu. If you don’t trust the file, don’t open it.)
  5. System Preferences will open into the Screen Saver section. Click ‘Install’ to complete.
  6. Select the screen saver from the list to the left to preview or enable it. You may need to scroll down to the end of your list to find it.
  7. Select ‘Screen Saver Options’ to select size of the numbers on your screen.
  8. Enjoy!
